# Objectives

## Primary Goal

Establish credibility and understand their environment well enough to propose a specific, relevant next step.

## Secondary Goals

- Understand their current CI/CD toolchain and where Ansible/AAP fits today (or doesn't)
- Surface any active pain points around infrastructure automation in their pipeline
- Introduce EDA as a concept if they aren't aware of it
- Leave them with at least one concrete resource they'll actually use

## What Success Looks Like

- They agree to a follow-up with a specific scope: demo, architecture review, or POC definition
- OR we learn enough to disqualify or redirect (also valuable — don't force fit)

## What We Want to Learn

1. What CI/CD toolchain are they running today? (Jenkins, GitLab CI, GitHub Actions, Tekton, other?)
2. How does infrastructure automation fit into their current pipeline — is it automated at all?
3. Are they using Ansible today? At what scale? Managed or ad hoc?
4. What is the specific pain or gap that prompted this conversation?
5. Who else is involved in evaluating this — architects, security, platform team?
6. What does their timeline look like — is there a project or deadline driving this?
